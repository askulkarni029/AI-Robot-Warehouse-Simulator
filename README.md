# 🤖 AI-Robot Warehouse Simulator

# Project Overview

An AI-generated robotics simulation demonstrating autonomous warehouse intralogistics operations.
The project shows a warehouse robot transporting a package, navigating to a rack, and placing the box on the shelf.
The entire video was created using AI image generation and video generation prompts, simulating a real-world warehouse automation workflow.

# 🖼️ Image Prompt — Robot Walking with Box

# Fig 1 :- 

A photorealistic modern logistics warehouse interior with tall industrial shelving racks filled with cardboard boxes. Bright overhead LED strip lighting illuminates the warehouse and reflects on a polished blue-gray concrete floor with yellow safety lane markings. In the center of the aisle, a sleek white humanoid warehouse robot with glowing blue LED accents and articulated mechanical hands is walking forward while carrying a cardboard box with both hands. The robot is moving down the warehouse aisle toward storage racks in the distance. # 🎬 Video Generation Prompt -

A cinematic warehouse automation sequence showing a humanoid robot performing an intralogistics task.
First, the humanoid warehouse robot walks steadily down a modern warehouse aisle while carrying a cardboard box with both hands. Tall industrial shelving racks filled with packages extend along both sides of the aisle under bright overhead LED lighting. The polished concrete floor with yellow safety lane markings reflects the lights as the robot walks.
Then, the robot approaches a storage rack and gradually slows down, stopping directly in front of an empty shelf compartment. The camera smoothly tracks the robot from the side, maintaining a professional industrial robotics demonstration perspective.
Finally, the robot turns slightly toward the rack and carefully extends both mechanical arms forward to place the cardboard box onto the empty shelf. After placing the box securely on the rack, the robot retracts its arms and stands beside the shelf.
Cinematic camera movement, smooth robotic motion, realistic warehouse lighting, ultra-realistic industrial environment, shallow depth of field, professional robotics automation showcase.
